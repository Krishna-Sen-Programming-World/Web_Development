Top three places you must visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, is an Islamic ivory-white marble mausoleum . It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favourite wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

Colosseum

The Colosseum in Rome, Italy, is a large amphitheater that hosted events like gladiatorial games. Design Pics Inc. The Colosseum, is a large amphitheater in Rome.

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is believed to have been built by Pachacuti Inca Yupanqui, the ninth ruler of the Inca during mid-1400s and stretched from Ecuador to Chile.
